# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as maintainers and contributors pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Trolling, insults, and personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.
